What data we collect?

Our web site don't collect any Session Cookies or other kind of cookies from which we (Marko93 Gaming) can collect any data from you. That means that our site don't put any cookies that you don't need.

Only cookies that our site have is from our newsletter provider - ConvertKit and from Google reCaptcha platform which is included with our newsletter subcription platform.
